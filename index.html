MERGING TYPOGRAPHIES

Located at the edge of the San Francisco city's grid where city meets the Bay, our concept will ultimately aim to express the boundaries condition of the site. There will be a merging condition where the existing city grids within the site reach the Embacarderro in order to provide different experiences for the users to enjoy the Bay in a whole new different way.
